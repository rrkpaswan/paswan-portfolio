About Me

I am a globally experienced researcher and data scientist with a PhD in Organisational Behaviour and Human Resource Management. Over the past five years, I have developed expertise in data analysis, social innovation, and ethical business practices, working with renowned institutions such as the European Commission in Belgium, the University of Porto in Portugal, and Universidad de las Américas Puebla in Mexico.

My passion lies in empowering individuals and organizations through data-driven strategies that drive sustainable and ethical growth. As a mentor, educator, and leader, I have shared my insights through publications and international conferences, striving to make a meaningful impact by bridging innovation with action.
